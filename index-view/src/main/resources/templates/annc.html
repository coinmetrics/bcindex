    November 26th, 2018

    Bitcoin Cash Satoshi's Vision (BCHSV) has been added with equal float to Bitcoin Cash ABC (BCH) to our 10, Total, and Currency Indexes. We have waited until there is adaquate liquidity and multiple of our quality exchanges allowing deposits of BCHSV to best represent what price investors could safely realize if liquidating the asset. There will be a jump in the price of each index reflecting the additional value reflected in this coin, and BCHSV will go through our normal eligibility requirements for the December rebalance.

    We are switching our source for the market capitilzation, and float percentages, of crypto assets from coinmarketcap.com to onchainfx.com. This is in accordance with our view that the typical market caps the community has been using are flawed, and a switch to fully diluted market cap is necessary. We applaud onchainfx for rising to fill that need.

    This change will result primarily in zcash and stellar lumens being in the ten index. It will also make ethereum classic, maidsafecoin, and gnosis much higher on the selection list than before. Weightings will be relatively unaffected, as the publicly available supply is consistent between the two sources.

    Additionally, we are adding a new rule to our general methodology. We are adding a float percentage requirement (or % available supply) of 10% to prevent the inclusion of assets that are extremely centralized. This requirement currently only excludes NEO GAS.

    Also starting with the October rebalance we will no longer be updating the tables in the methodology section with the weightings at each rebalance. You can instead find a link to those on our updates page. We will leave the tables up to help provide historical context.
